I. ISESENGURA RY’IBYAGEZWEHO
1.1 Intangiriro
Inama y’Igihugu y’Umushyikirano iteganywa n’ingingo ya 168 y’Itegeko Nshinga
ryo ku wa 4 Kamena 2003 nk’uko ryavuguruwe kugeza ubu. Iterana buri mwaka
iyobowe na Nyakubahwa Perezida wa Repubulika. Ihuza Nyakubahwa Perezida
wa Repubulika n’abahagarariye Inama Njyanama za buri Karere n’Umujyi wa Kigali
batoranywa na bagenzi babo. Itumirwamo kandi Abagize Guverinoma, abagize
Inteko Ishinga Amategeko imitwe yombi n’abandi bagenwa na Nyakubahwa
Perezida wa Repubulika.
Inama y’Igihugu y’Umushyikirano iganira ku bibazo bireba Igihugu, ibibazo bireba
Inzego z’Ibanze n’ubumwe bw’Abanyarwanda. Imyanzuro ifatwa (mu mibereho
myiza y’abaturage, mu miyoborere myiza, mu butabera no mu bukungu) ishyikirizwa
inzego ireba kugira ngo ishyirwe mu bikorwa. Inama y’Igihugu y’Umushyikirano
ni bumwe mu buryo Nyakubahwa Perezida wa Repubulika ahura n’abahagarariye
abaturage bakaganira ku bibazo biri mu gihugu bigashakirwa ibisubizo. Iyi nama
ni kimwe mu bipimo bigaragaza uburyo abaturage bagira uruhare mu ifatwa
ry’ibyemezo bireba imicungire y’igihugu.

1.2 Incamake ya raporo
 Uko imyanzuro yari iteye
Imyanzuro 6 yafashwe ku Iterambere ry’ubukungu yibanze ku gukangurira
abaturage kugana Umurenge SACCO, kurinda ubutaka bwo mu gishanga himurwa
abantu bose bahituje no gukemura mu buryo bwihuse ibibazo bikiri mu bucuruzi
bw’ibikomoka ku buhinzi n’ubworozi”.
Mu miyoborere myiza hafashwe imyanzuro 6 igamije kurushaho kunoza ingamba
zo gukumira Jenoside hitabwa ku mibereho y’abacitse ku icumu rya Jenoside
yakorewe Abatutsi no kubungabunga ibimenyetso bya jenoside, kunoza politiki
igena ikanatunganya ibikorwa by’abakorerabushake mu Rwanda, gukomeza
kwegereza abaturage ubuyobozi n’ubushobozi, gukumira ikibazo cya ruswa
kivugwa mu nzego z’Ibanze no gushishikariza Abanyarwanda bakiri mu buhungiro
gutaha mu gihugu cyabo.
Mu mibereho myiza y’abaturage hafashwe imyanzuro 7 yerekeranye no gushyira
no kongera ingufu mu kwigisha ururimi rw’Ikinyarwanda, Umuco na Siporo, guca
burundu inyubako zigayitse za nyakatsi, gushishikariza abikorera gushora imari mu
burezi n’ababyeyi kugira uruhare rukomeye mu myigire y’abana babo, kongera
ubwinshi n’ubushobozi bwa TVET, gutangiza vuba Inteko y’Ururimi n’Umuco,
gutegura gahunda yo kwinjira muri gahunda y’Uburezi bw’imyaka 12 no kongera
ingufu muri gahunda z’ubuzima.
Mu butabera hafashwe imyanzuro 4 yerekeranye no kurwanya abantu bamunga
ubukungu bw’Igihugu; ababigizemo uruhare bagakurikiranwa mu nkiko, kuvugurura
amategeko arebana n’ubwiteganyirize bw’izabukuru kugira ngo bugere ku bantu
bose harimo n’abadafite umushahara wa buri kwezi no gukorana n’ibihugu

by’amahanga kugira ngo abakoze Jenoside baba muri ibyo bihugu boherezwe mu
Rwanda bacirirwe imanza cyangwa baburanishwe mu bihugu barimo.
 Ibyagezweho by’ingenzi
Mu Iterambere ry’Ubukungu, abanyamuryango b’Imirenge SACCO bariyongereye,
bavuye kuri 1,155,917 bagera kuri 1,354,748. Imirenge SACCO 90 yahawe icyemezo
cya burundu gitangwa na BNR, naho 340 ifite icyemezo cy’agateganyo kiyemerera
gukora (kwakira amafaranga yo kubitsa ariko baba bataremererwa gutanga
inguzanyo). Ku bijyanye n’inyubako 4.501 zabaruwe mu bishanga, 567 zimaze
kwimurwa, igikorwa kiracyakomeza. Mu rwego rwo kurengera abahinzi, MINAGRI
itangaza ibiciro by’imyaka kuri televiziyo, radiyo no ku rubuga rwa interineti
(internet) www.e-soko.gov.rw; kandi MINAGRI yatanze amabwiriza asaba ibigo bya
Leta nk’amashuri na za gereza kujya bigura ibiribwa mu makoperative nibura ku
kigereranyo cya 40% y’ibyo bakeneye.
Mu miyoborere myiza, intumwa za Minisiteri ishinzwe Gucyura Impunzi no Gucunga
Ibiza zasuye impunzi z’Abanyarwanda ziba muri Zambia, Malawi, Zimbabwe, na
Congo Brazzaville, zishishikarizwa kugaruka mu gihugu. Ibi byatumye impunzi
98 zitahuka. Hatangiye icyiciro cya gatatu cya gahunda yo kwegereza ubuyobozi
n’ubushobozi abaturage (2011-2015). Hashyizweho kandi itsinda rigizwe n’abantu
baturutse muri MINIPRESIREP, MINALOC n’Ibiro by’Umuvunyi kugira ngo bige ku
bibazo bya ruswa bivugwa mu nzego z’Ibanze.
Mu mibereho myiza, hashyizweho amabwiriza № 411/12.00/2011 yo ku wa
18/02/2011 yo kunoza imyigire n’imyigishirize y’Ikinyarwanda, ubugororangingo
na siporo mu mashuri abanza n’ayisumbuye. Ingengo y’imari ya Siporo n’Umuco
mu mashuri yariyongereye iva kuri 253,940,295 Frw igera kuri 560,075,563 Frw.
Abagore bitabira kuboneza urubyaro bageze kuri 45% (2011) mu gihe bari 27% muri
2008.
Mu butabera, havuguruwe amategeko anyuranye arebana n’umurimo n’agena
ibirebana n’ubwiteganyirize. Ku birebana no guhana abakekwaho kuba barakoze
Jenoside baba mu mahanga, ibihugu nk’Ubuholandi, Ububiligi n’Ubufaransa hari
10
rwego rw’ubutabera, urutonde rw’abamunga ubukungu bw’igihugu rwarakozwe
rushyikirizwa inzego zibishinzwe.
Ku myanzuro 23 yafashwe, 19 (83%) yashyizwe mu bikorwa mu buryo bushimishije
(irangwa n’ibara ry’icyatsi kibisi) naho 4 (17 %) yashyizwe mu bikorwa mu buryo

mu bikorwa wose cyangwa igice kinini cyawo. Nta mwanzuro uri mu ibara
ry’umutuku. Imbonerahamwe ikurikira irerekana uko imyanzuro 23 yashyizwe mu
bikorwa.

1.4 Uko imyanzuro yashyizwe mu bikorwa muri buri nkingi
a) Iterambere ry’ubukungu
Hemejwe imyanzuro 6 mu iterambere ry’ubukungu. Muri yo 4 yashyizwe mu
bikorwa ku buryo bushimishije (hejuru ya 90%) naho 2 ishyirwa mu bikorwa
mu buryo buciriritse (hagati ya 50% na 89%). Mu byagezweho bishimishije hari
ukwitabira Umurenge SACCO cyane cyane abagore kuko ubu bageze kuri 38.6%
by’abanyamuryango bose ba za SACCOs. Imbonerahamwe ikurikira irerekana uko
imyanzuro yashyizwe mu bikorwa mu iterambere ry’ubukungu.
