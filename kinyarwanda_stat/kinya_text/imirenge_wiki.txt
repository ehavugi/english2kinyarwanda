Imirenge y’u Rwanda


Imirenge y’u Rwanda ni 416 [1]. Umurenge uyoborwa n’umunyamabanga-nshingwabikorwa, afatanije n’inama-njyanama ishyirwaho binyuze mu matora. Iyi nama-njyanama ifite inshingano zo gushyira mu bikorwa gahunda za Leta nk’uko bikorwa mu karere, hakiyongeraho gufasha abaturage kwigeza ku iterambere.
