Inyandiko
www.urumuri.com,ni igitangazamakuru gikorera kuri internet gifite intego nyamukuru yo kuba umuyoboro ukomeye wifashishwa mu Gusobanura no Kumenyekanisha ibikorwa,hatangazwa amakuru anyuranye hagamijwe guteza imbere abagisoma bamenya ibiriho n’ibigezweho . Mu ntego zacu kandi harimo guhanga imirimo mishya cyane cyane mubakiri bato,habyazwa amahirwe ariho ndetse naboneka hafi yaho baherereye,ndetse hakoreshejwe ikoranabuhanga . Dufasha abafite ubumenyi ubwo aribwo bwose,kubugeza kw’isoko ry’umurimo,no kuzamura impano zitandukanye. Dufasha abafite ibyo bakora bifuza kumenyekanisha no kubisobanura,tubakorera inkuru,gutegura no gutunganya ibiganiro binyura kuri radio,n’ubundi buryo bwo kw’amamaza binyuze kuri internet no mubindi bitangazamakuru(media production).
