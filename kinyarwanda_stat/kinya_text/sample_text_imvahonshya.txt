Muri iyi nama rero twaganiriye ku mutekano, aho twavuze ku byaha byambukiranya imipaka nk’icuruzwa ry’abantu cyane cyane abana b’abakobwa, ubujura bukoresheje ikoranabuhanga, iterabwoba, n’ibindi harimo nko koneshereza imyaka ku baturage bo ku mipaka yombi.” Yunzemo ati “Gusa muri rusange abaturage b’ibihugu byombi rwose babanye neza k’uburyo iyo havutse akabazo gato nk’ako twavuze ko konesha imyaka abaturage babiganiraho bigakemuka neza kandi mu mahoro”. AIGP Dr.Fred Yiga wari uyoboye intumwa za Polisi ya Uganda, nawe yagize ati “Nabonye ikintu gikomeye, aho Perezida avuga ko abanyarwanda bagomba kumva ko umutekano ari uburenganzira bwabo, iki kintu cyantangaje  cyane, ni ikintu ngiye gutwara iwacu, kandi iyi nama yakivuzeho. Mu byo twaganiriye kandi twarebeye hamwe uko twarushaho kubungabunga umutekano hagati y’abanyarwanda n’abagande, k’uburyo bagirira icyizere inzego z’umutekano”. Iyi nama yabaye mu gihe abanyarwanda bakora ubucuruzi bwambukiranya umupaka wa Uganda bishimira ko basigaye bakora bisanzuye, kuko umutekano wabo usigaye ari ntamakemwa. Uzamukunda Anisie, umwe mu bagore bakora ubucuruzi bwambukiranya umupaka, yagize ati “Ubu umutekano w’u Rwanda na Uganda ni mwiza cyane, nkatwe b’abacuruzi rwose usanga tujya kurangura muri Uganda, nta kibazo ko dushobora kwamburwa ibyacu, abagande kandi nabo ni uko mu Rwanda barisanzura kuko hari n’abaza kubaka inzu zaba izo kubamo ndetse n’iz’ubucuruzi. Ibi bigaragaza ko umutekano rwose abaturiye ibi bihugu tuwizeye 100%, ikindi nuko kuri ubu kwambukira ku indangamuntu byaje bikongera ubuhahirane bw’ibi bihugu aho umuntu yambukira igihe ashakaye, akagaruka nta nkomyi, mu gihe mu myaka yashize no kugira ngo mu Rwanda rwacu uve mu ntara ujye mu yindi byagusabaha urupapuro rw’inzira, ibi kandi nanone kuri njye navuga ko biterwa n’imiyoborere myiza ibi bihugu bifite”. Aba baturage basaba inzego zishinzwe umutekano mu bihugu byose gukomeza kunoza imikoranire, kugira ngo umutekano ukomeze gusigasirwa, na cyane ko bahamya ko ari wo shingiro ry’iterambere bamaze kugeraho. Ibi Senateri Tito Rutaremara yabitangarije Imvaho Nshya mu kiganiro kihariye bagiranye, ubwo yamubazaga byinshi ku birebana no kwibohora ku nshuro ya 23, umunsi u Rwanda rwizihiza uyu munsi ku wa 4 Nyakanga 2017. Urugamba rwo kubohora igihugu rwasojwe ku wa 4 Nyakanga 1994,  ruyobowe na Perezida Paul Kagame, ari na bwo ingabo yari ayoboye zahoze ari iza FPR-Inkotanyi zahagaritse Jenoside yakorewe Abatutsi. Tito Rutaremara, yagize ati “Urugamba rujya gutangira, abantu babanje kureba impamvu nyakuri zituma habaho uru rugamba rwo kubohora u Rwanda n’Abanyarwanda. Impamvu nyakuri zari uko ubumwe bw’Abanyarwanda bwari bwarasenyutse, ubukene bukabije, ubujiji, kutishyira no kwizana kw’Abanyarwanda, ruswa n’itonesha.”    Senateri Tito Rutaremara ahamya ko intego yatumye FPR itangiza urugamba rwo kubohora igihugu yagezweho (Foto James R.)  Yavuze ko kutishyira ukizana kw’Abanyarwanda byatumaga nta we uva muri Komini ngo ajye mu yindi, nta wageraga i Kigali adafite urupapuro rw’inzira. Senateri Rutaremara avuga kandi ko nta bwigenge Abanyarwanda bari bafite muri Demokarasi, kuko nta matora yabagaho, Abanyarwanda bategekeshwaga igitugu kandi ntibagiraga n’ubwisanzure mu gutanga ibitekerezo byabo. Avuga ko FPR-Inkotanyi yashinzwe mu 1987 igamije kubohora Abanyarwanda izo ngoyi zose zari zibaziritse, kandi ngo yabigezeho. Yagize ati “N’ubwo urugamba rwo kwibohora rugikomeje, ariko hari aho u Rwanda rwavuye n’aho rugeze rubikesha gahunda nziza y’Umuryango RPF-Inkotanyi n’abantu bashoboye kwitanga mu buryo bw’abakorerabushake.” Senateri Rutaremara yavuze ko kubohora u Rwanda byatangijwe n’urugamba rwa gisirikare kuko izindi nzira zari zarananiranye. Avuga ko urugamba rutarwanywe n’abasirikare gusa, ko kuko rwari urw’abantu bose, barimo urubyiruko, ababyeyi, abaturage n’abandi kuko bafashije mu buryo bunyuranye. Yagize ati “Abakecuru n’abasaza babaga bari mu buhungiro ntibicaye, bakusanyaga utwo bafite bakaduhuza bakohorereza abari ku rugamba rw’amasasu, barimo abana babo, abagabo babo, inshuti n’abandi. Icyo gihe rero barwanaga na bo urugamba mu buryo bwabo, abasirikare na bo bakarurwana mu buryo bw’amasasu.”   Ubuyobozi bushyikirana n’abaturage, Umukuru w’Igihugu Paul Kagame agahura na bo, bakaganira ku cyerekezo cy’igihugu n’uko cyagerwaho  Senateri Rutaremara kandi yemeza ko intego ya FPR-Inkotanyi yagezweho kuko icya mbere cyari ukubanza kwigizayo igitugu, ishyiraho ubuyobozi bubereye Abanyarwanda, igarura ubumwe bw’Abanyarwanda, ishyiraho Inkiko Gacaca n’ibindi. Abanyarwanda basubijwe ubwigenge na FPR Senateri Rutaremara avuga ko FPR igeze kure igera ku ntego zayo kuko irimo kurwanya ingoyi y’ubukene n’ubujiji mu banyarwanda, amashuri y’uburezi bw’ibanze bw’imyaka icyenda na cumi n’ibiri yagiyeho, ubu abana b’u Rwanda bose bariga nta vangura iryo ari ryo ryose. Rutaremara agaragaza kandi uburyo mu Rwanda hari ukwishyira ukizana kw’Abanyarwanda, aho bavuga ibibabangamiye, ibitagenda mu gihugu kandi bakabibwira Perezida wa Repubulika ubwe. Yagize ati “Kuri ubu Abanyarwanda basubijwe ubwigenge bwo kugaragaraza uko babona ibintu bari barambuwe na Leta z’igitugu. Bahura na Perezida wa Repubulika bakamuregera abayobozi b’uturere, ba Minisitiri n’abandi batabakorera uko bikwiye.” Aha Rutaremara anenga abantu bakivuga ko mu Rwanda nta bwisanzure buhari, aho asanga abavuga ibyo ari ukwirengagiza ukuri babishaka, kuko iyo abaturage baregera Perezida w’igihugu abayobozi babakorera ibitari byo, abantu bose baba babibona. Ati “Ubundi ibyo nta ho byabaye ku isi, ni umwihariko w’u Rwanda kuko rufite  ubuyobozi bwiza buha abaturage ijambo, bukabakemurira ibibazo, bukarwanya akarengane na ruswa.”   Ubwisanzure mu banyarwanda, bagaragariza Perezida Paul Kagame ibibazo byabo, aho barega n’abayobozi batuzuje inshingano. Aha abaturage bagezaga ibibazo byabo kuri Perezida Kagame, ni mu karere ka Karongi yabasuye  Haracyari ibindi byo kwibohora birimo ubukene Senateri Tito Rutaremara avuga ko nubwo hari aho intego za FPR zagezweho, inzira yo kwibohora irakomeza. Avuga ko ingoyi zikomeje guhamburwa, nk’ingoyi y’ubukene ni urugamba rukomeje, kubohora Abanyarwanda ingoyi y’ubujiji na rwo ni urugamba rugikomeza. Avuga ko mu rugamba rwo kurandura ingoyi y’ubukene hashyizweho gahunda yo guhanga imirimo myinshi kandi ihabwa benshi, bityo ngo abana basoje amashuri yabo bakabasha kubona icyo bakora. Yagize ati “N’ubwo dukomeje guhambura ingoyi ziboshye Abanyarwanda, haracyari abakiboshye, ni urugamba rukomeza kandi twizeye kuzarutsinda.” Avuga ko urugamba rw’amasasu ari rwo rwabimburiye kwibohora k’u Rwanda, ariko ntibivuze ko kwibohora byarangiye. FPR ntiyikubiye ubutegetsi kuko ishyize imbere Demukarasi Senateri Tito Rutaremara agaragaza uburyo FPR imaze gutsinda urugamba itikubiye ubutegetsi, ahubwo yabusaranganyije n’andi mashyaka ya politiki mu rwego rwo gufatanya mu kuyobora igihugu. Avuga ko politiki yashyizweho na FPR-Inkotanyi yo gusaranganya ubutegetsi nta mpungenge iteye kandi ngo nta n’imbogamizi zikomeye yagize. Ati “Gusangira ubutegetsi n’abandi ni ugushimangira demokarasi, kuko ari byo FPR yashyize imbere. Uwatsindiye ubutegetsi agabana n’abandi, iyo ni yo ntego yacu nka FPR.” Asobanura  ko uburyo bwo kuyobora buri mu Rwanda ari uko abaturage bose bagira ububasha bwo kuyobora, uhereye mu nzego z’ibanze, amashyaka ya Politiki na yo yatsinze amatora ahabwa imyanya muri Politiki hagendewe ku ntsinzi yabonye. Senateri Rutaremara kandi agaruka ku Ihuriro ry’imitwe ya Politiki, nk’urubuga amashyaka ya Politiki ahuriramo akaganiriramo, bakumvikana ku cyakorwa mu kuyobora Abanyarwanda. Avuga ko Demokarasi atari uguhangana no kurwana, ko ahubwo Demokarasi ari ukwishyira ukizana mu bwumvikane bwuzuye, amahoro n’umutekano. Kuba FPR-Inkotanyi yaranenze Demokarasi yariho mbere y’urugamba rwo kwibohora, kuri ubu ngo igishimishije ni uko ibyari bibangamiye Abanyarwanda byakosowe kandi bikomeje gukosorwa. Komisiyo y’Ubumwe n’Ubwiyunge ikomoka ku masezerano y’Arusha Senateri Tito Rutaremara avuga hari ingingo zikubiye mu masezerano y’Arusha zifashishwa mu kuyobora igihugu. Yatanze urugero rwa Komisiyo y’Ubumwe n’Ubwiyunge ifite inkomoko mu masezerano y’Arusha n’ubwo bwose yavuguruwe mu biganiro byo mu Rugwiro. Ati “Burya mubona, Komisiyo y’Igihugu y’Ubumwe n’Ubwiyunge ikomoka ku masezerano y’Arusha. Twatanze icyo cyifuzo nka FPR ubwo twari mu masezerano y’Arusha, kiremerwa, kandi n’ubu murabona ko ubumwe n’ubwiyunge buganje mu gihugu.” Avuga ko kuri ubu ubumwe n’ubwiyunge mu banyarwanda bugeze kuri 92%, akaba ari intambwe ishimishije n’ubwo itaragera ku 100%, ariko hari aho bigeze. Senateri Rutaremara kandi avuga ko Politiki nziza ya FPR ishingiye ku bumwe bw’Abanyarwanda yanafashije gucyura impunzi zose zari hanze, nta vangura. Ati “Politiki ya FPR yageze ku ntego yo gucyura Abanyarwanda benshi bari hanze y’igihugu kandi ibikora itavanguye. Icyo gihe hari impunzi zasagaga miliyoni eshatu hanze, iza kera n’iza vuba.”   Urugamba rwo kubaka igihugu rurakomeje, harwanywa ubukene, hubakwa iterambere buri munyarwanda yibonamo  Avuga ko FPR ifatanyije na Leta y’Ubumwe bw’Abanyarwanda yabashije gucyura impunzi nyinshi, ibintu bidakunze kubaho, kuko ubundi bimenyerewe ko impunzi zicyurwa na UNHCR. Ati “Ni twe gihugu twabashije gucyura impunzi LONI itadufashije, kandi twabigezeho, n’ubwo hari abacyinangiriye banga gutaha mu gihugu.” Rutaremara asoza avuga ko intego FPR yari ifite itangiza urugamba rwo kwibohora zigenda zigerwaho. Avuga ko kurwanya ubukene ari inzira ikomeza, ariko ko aho bigeze hashimishije, kugarura ubumwe bw’Abanyarwanda na byo bigeze ahashimishije, kurandura ubujiji mu banyarwanda na byo biri mu nzira nziza, imibereho myiza y’abaturage na yo igeze ku ntera nziza, gucyura impunzi na byo biri mu murongo mwiza, ariko ngo  ibyo byose birakomeza. Ati “Turashaka ko kuri  ayo 19 haziyongeraho bya bigo by’amashuri dushaka kwifashisha  mu kumenyekanisha no gutoza abana gukina kung Fu, ko iyo  watangiye ukiri muto nibwo  ugaragaza impano”. Avuga ku bijyanye n’ubuke bw’amarushwa, Perezida w’ishyirahamwe ry’umukino wa Kungu Fu Wushu mu Rwanda, Uwiragiye Marc yemeje ko ari  ikibazo  kuko bisaba ubushobozi batari bagira.  Aha yatanze urugero  rw’irushanwa ry’imikino y’Afurika izabera muri Benin  kuva tariki 19 kugeza 24 Nyakanga 2017 aho bifuza kujyana amakipe abiri agizwe n’abakinnyi 8, 4 mu kwiyereka “Taulu” no kurwana “Sanda” ariko ko  basanze byatwara hafi miliyoni 40 z’amafaranga y’u Rwanda. Ati “Bisaba ubushobozi twe uyu munsi tudafite ari nayo mpampavu tubasaba inkuga ngo twitabire kuko  bizadufasha kumenya urwego turiho.  Niba  turi ku rwego rwiza  tuzane imidali kandi nizera ko bizadufasha gutera imbere tukava ku rwego  rw’Afurika tukajya  ku rwego rw’isi”. Yakomeje agira ati “Ntiwamenya aho uhagaze utigeze wipima, turasaba Minisiteri y’umuco na Siporo kudufasha  tukagenda tukamenya aho duhagaze  natwe tujye mu mikino isohoka tugaragaze  impano n’ubushobozi dufite”. Avuga ko basabye inkunga MINISPOC bagitegereje igisubizo. Ati “Ntabwo baradusubiza gusa muri iyi minsi turizera ko bazatubwira.Ubu  ikipe irimo gukora imyitozo ariko kuva taliki  3 kugeza 15 Nyakanga 2017  nibwo tuzakora imyitozo nyayo. Ubu dukorera Kimisagara”. Uwiragiye avuga ko MINISPOC nitagira icyo ibafasha nta kindi bakora kuko nta bushobozi bafite kandi nta bandi baterankunga bahari. Incamake ku buzima bwa Uwiragiye Marc Perezida  mushya w’ishyirahamwe ry’umukino wa Kung Fu Wushu , Uwiragiye Marc yavutse taliki  14 Mutarama 1972, avukira ahitwa Ngeruka mu Karere ka Bugesera. Arubatse afite umugore n’abana 6 harimo 4 be n’abandi 2 arera. Yatangiye gukina uyu mukino wa Kung Fu mu 1987. Mu 1990 ni bwo  hatangijwe ikipe ya mbere itangijwe n’uwitwa Rutayisire Jean Marie Vianney muri St Famille gusa ntabwo yayikinnyemo . Nyuma ya Jenoside yakorewe Abatutsi muri Mata 1994 abari barokotse bongeye kubyutsa ikipe. Mu 1997,Uwiragiye yatangiye gutoza uyu mukino anakina. Kuri ubu afite impamyabushobozi yo gutoza ku rwego rw’isi yakuye mu Bushinwa  aho mu Rwanda ubu hari abatoza 4 gusa bazifite. Uwiragiye ni Injeniyeri “Engineer” mu bijyanye n’ikoranabuhanga mu itumanaho ubu akaba afite sosiyete “UMARCOTEC Ltd” ikora ibijyanye n’ikoranabuhanga.
