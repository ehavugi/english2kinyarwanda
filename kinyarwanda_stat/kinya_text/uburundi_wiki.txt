République du Burundi
Burundi n'igihugu muri Afurika rw'Iburasirazuba.Ibihugu bitatu bihana imbibi ni Rwanda mu majyaruguru,Tanzania mu burasirazuba na mu majyepfo,Repubulika iharanira Demokarasi ya Kongo mu burengerazuba.Umurwa mukuru rw'ikigihugu ni Bujumbura.Ururimi rw'Abarundi ni kirundi.Abaturage babarirwa hafi ya 10,216,190,ubwo rero n’abantu 367 kuri buri kilometero karre.
