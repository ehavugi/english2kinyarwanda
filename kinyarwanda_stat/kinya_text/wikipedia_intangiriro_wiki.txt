Indimi Wikipediya ibonekamo
Iyi Wikipediya yanditswe mu Kinyarwanda. Kuva yatangira mu 2001, ifite kugeza ubu inyandiko zigera kuri 1,806 articles. Hariho izindi Wikipediya nyinshi; zimwe muri zo zigaragaraho kuba nini cyane zagaragajwe hasi aha.
