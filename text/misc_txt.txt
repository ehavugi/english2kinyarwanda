 Gukoresha ikinyarwanda cy’umwuga mu kumva, kuvuga, gusoma no kwandika, mu bikorwa bijyanye n’umwuga we.

 Gukoresha neza indangahantu.
 Kwandika yubahiriza ifatana n’itandukana ry’amagambo.

INGINGO N’IBIPIMO BY’UBUSHOBOZI
Ingingo z’ubushobozi zisobanura umusaruro w’ibanze ugomba kugerwaho.

Uwiga ushoje iyi mbumbanyigisho aba ashoboye :
IBIPIMO BY’UBUSHOBOZI
1. Gukoresha ubuvanganzo gakondo bufatiye ku
mwuga ashyikirana n’abandi
1.1. Gukoresha Ikinyarwanda kiboneye uwiga agaragaza ko yumva ubuvanganzo
gakondo bufatiye ku mwuga abinyujije mu ngiro zitandukanye.
1.2. Gusubiza mu mvugo iboneye ibibazo ku buvanganzo gakondo bufatiye ku
mwuga.
1.3. Gusoma neza umwandiko w’ubuvanganzo gakondo bufatiye ku mwuga
yubahiriza utwatuzo n’isesekaza.
1.4. Guhimba ahuza n’ubuvanganzo gakondo bufatiye ku mwuga akurikiranya neza
ingingo.
1.5. Gutarama akoresheje ubuvanganzo bwizwe.
2. Gukoresha Ikinyarwanda kiboneye agaragaza
ibyiza by’ikoranabuhanga mu iterambere
ry’umwuga no kugaragaza intego y’izina
mbonera.
2.1. Gukoresha Ikinyarwanda kiboneye, uwiga agaragaza ko yumva umwandiko ku
nsanganyamatsiko y’ikoranabuhanga n’iterambere ry’umwuga abinyujije mu
ngiro zinyuranye.

Ijambo ry’Ibanze
Mbere y’ubukoroni, Ikinyarwanda cyari umuyoboro ufasha Abanyarwanda
gusangira ubumenyi, kikanababera igikoresho cy’ibanze cy’umushyikirano
n’imitegekere y’Igihugu.
Ikinyarwanda cyarindwaga ibicyonona, utandukiriye bakamugarura mu nzira. Aha
ni ho havuye “Ntibavuga – Bavuga”, aho wasangaga ushyomye bamucyaha,
bigatoza umwana w’u Rwanda gukura avuga Ikinyarwanda kiboneye, mu yandi
magambo adashyoma.
Muri iki gihe na ho, ururimi rwacu ruracyafite agaciro gakomeye mu mibereho
yacu. Ikinyarwanda ni yo nzira y’ikubitiro y’uburere bukwiye umwana w’u Rwanda
kuko ari cyo kimusogongezaku mateka n’umuco by’Igihugu cyacu.
Ikinyarwanda si ururimi gusa; ni n’umuco w’u Rwanda, uko dutekereza, uko
twitwara n’uko dukemura ibibazo byacu. Ni yo mpamvu ujya wumva bavuga bati
“Ikinyarwanda cy’aha n’aha; i Bunaka bafite Ikinyarwanda cyabo...”. Baba
bashaka kuvuga umuco waho.
Igiteye impungenge muri iki gihe ariko ni uko umwimerere n’uburyohe
by’Ikinyarwanda bigenda bitokozwa no gushyoma. Gushyoma ni ugukoresha
imvugo itaboneye cyangwa iteshuka ku kibonezamvugo cy’Ikinyarwanda, ahanini
kubera ubumenyi buke mu rurimi.
Mu Kinyarwanda bumwe mu buryo abantu bakunze gushyomamo ni ugukoresha
nabi amabango atanaze cyangwa imvugo zidanangiye.Izo mvugo ni nk’imigani
y’imigenurano cyangwa imigani migufi n’inshoberamahanga.
Bene izo mvugo usanga zifite intego itanaze cyangwa imiterere idahinduka,
wayihindura zikavuga ibinyuranye n’ibyo zisanzwe zivuga. Hari n’abakoresha izo
mvugo mu bihe (imvugiro) bitari byo bakazitwerera ibyo zitavuga.
Tunoze Ikinyarwanda No 2iribanda ku migani itatu n’inshoberamahanga ebyiri
bikunze gukoreshwa nabi mu Kinyarwanda, ikerekana uburyo bunoze bigomba
gukoreshwamo.
Inteko Nyarwanda y’Ururimi n’Umuco irashishikariza buri wese kwihatira gukoresha
neza Ikinyarwanda, kucyiga no kugikundisha abandi kuko ari cyo gisigasiye umuco
wacu.

“Bamushakiye hasi kubura hejuru”
Dukurikije Inkoranyamagaambo y’Îkinyarwaanda mu Kinyarwaanda yatangajwe
n’Ikigo cyitwaga IRST mu wa 2011 (p. 201), iyi nshoberamahanga “gushakira
cyangwa guhigira hasi kubura hejuru” isobanura “gushaka umuntu cyangwa ikintu ku
buryo bwose; gushakira ahantu hose ushyizeho umwete”.
Iyi nshoberamahanga abantu bajya bibeshya ku miterere yayo aho kuvuga
‘‘gushakira hasi kubura hejuru“ bakavuga ‘‘gushakira kubura hasi kubura hejuru”.
Inshoberamahanga ni itsinda ry‘amagamboadanangiye ryitwara nk’ijambo rimwe.
Igisobanuro cyayo ntabwo kiba ari igiteranyo cy’ibisobanuro by’amagambo ayigize,

nk’uko bigenda ku yandi matsinda y’amagambo cyangwa ku nteruro. Yo ifatwa
nk’ijambo rimwe nubwo yaba igizwe n’amagambo menshi, ikagira igisobanuro kimwe
gishobora no kutagira aho gihurira n’ibisobanuro by’amagambo ayigize.
Iyo wongeyemo irindi jambo cyangwa ukagira iryo ukuramo ntiba ikivuga icyo
yavugaga. Iyi myubakire yongera ijambo ridasanzwe muri iyi nshoberamahanga
(gushakira kubura hasi kubura hejuru) ntikwiriye. Imivugire iboneye yayo ni
“gushakira hasi kubura hejuru”, urugero: “Bamushakiye hasi kubura hejuru ”.
Bavuga “ari mu mazi abira” cyangwa “ariho amazi abira”?
Iyo uvuze ko umuntu ari mu mazi abira uba ushaka kuvuga ko ari mu kaga cyangwa
mu makuba akomeye, bigoye kwikuramo.
Twagiye twumva abantu bamwe bakoresha iyi nshoberamahanga ku buryo
bugoretse, urugero bakavuga bati “Kanaka ariho amazi abira”. Iyi mvugo ntiboneye.
Imvugo iboneye ni “Kanakaari mu mazi abira”.
Inshoberamahanga igomba gukoreshwa uko iri idahinduriwe imiterere kuko
ubudahungabanywa bw’amagambo ayubatse ari bwo buyiha igisobanuro mu rurimi.
Umusozo
Nta kiryoshya ibiganiro mu Kinyarwanda nko kumenya gusobeka neza imigani
y‘imigenuranomu mvugo.
Icyakora kugira ngo imigani idufashe gushimangira ibitekerezo byacu ku buryo
buboneye, igomba gukoreshwa neza kuko ibisobanuro byayo bitakaraiyo amagambo
ayigize akurikiranyijwe uko umuntu abonye. Uko ni na ko bigenda ku
nshoberamahanga.
Ikindi kandi, buri mugani ugira imimerere (imvugiro) ukoreshwamo iba yaragenwe
n’ururimi. Dukwiye kuyimenya neza kugira ngo tutayitwerera ibyo itavuga.
Hari ibitabo n’inkoranyamagambo bikubiyemo ubwo bumenyi bw‘ururimi
n’ubuvanganzo bwacu, byabidufashamo. Tuzabigarukaho mu minsi iri imbere.
